Log's Info

Hi there! I'm Log, the creator behind Log's Cabin. I'm a passionate content creator with a love for tech and pets. I started this website as a way to share my projects, thoughts, and creations with the world, and to create a fun mini-game hub for my friends to chill at.

I go by many names—Log, LogChop, ChopLog, Choppy—but people mostly call me "Log".

When I'm not working on the website, you can find me playing games, watching anime, or just chilling on Discord. I believe in the balance of rest and work and strive to infuse that philosophy into everything I do.

Thank you for visiting Log's Cabin. I hope you find something here that inspires or entertains you. Feel free to reach out through the contact page if you'd like to connect, suggest a new game, or recommend a feature to add.
